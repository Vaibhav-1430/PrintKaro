Our mission

Campus printing, finally effortless.

Every student knows the pain: a looming deadline, a USB stick, a walk to the library, and a printer that's out of paper. Print Karo exists to make that whole ordeal disappear.

Upload from anywhere, pay in seconds, and collect your print with a secure one-time PIN. We health-check every machine so you never pay for a printer that can't deliver — and your documents stay private, downloaded to the machine only after your PIN is verified and deleted right after.

We're building the printing layer for campuses: fast, fair, and beautifully simple.
